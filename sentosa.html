More about Resorts World Sentosa - Beach Villas

More about Resorts World Sentosa - Beach Villas overview

Located in Sentosa, Resorts World Sentosa - Beach Villas is merely seven kilometers away from the city center. The hotel is also a further 25 minutes away from the airport. Resorts World Sentosa - Beach Villas is close to entertainment areas such as Adventure Cove at Dolphin Island, Butterfly Park & Insect Kingdom, and Sentosa 4D Magix. The hotel is designed to create an atmosphere for an idyllic retreat, with lavish furnishings to complement the hotel settings. Each room at the hotel is designed to create peace and harmony for guests and providing necessary amenities. At Resorts World Sentosa - Beach Villas, guests can also enjoy relaxing views of nature and glittering waters. Guest will also be able to enjoy access to the facilities at ESPA Resorts World Sentosa, which includes a Vitality Pool, Forest Onsen-style Pool, Rock Sauna ,and Crystal Steam Room.
