More about Studio M Hotel

Studio M Hotel overview

Studio M Hotel is Singapore’s first fully loft-inspired hotel where style and functionality are key. Ranging from Studio to Moonlight Lofts, step into the comfort of the urban-inspired and stylish rooms with 3-meter high ceiling and sleek amenities. Whether you’re travelling for work, leisure or a bit of good-old self-discovery, you can do so in style in a hotel that was crowned AsiaOne People’s Choice Award for best Boutique Hotel in 2014 & 15. The property is situated in the bustling entertainment precinct of Robertson Quay, just minutes away from the Central Business District (CBD) and renowned Orchard Road. You won’t have to venture far to seek new thrills, make your business deals, or taste the mouth-watering cuisine of Singapore while staying at Studio M Hotel.
