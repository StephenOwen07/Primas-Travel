More about Hotel ICON

Hotel ICON overview

Offering quality accommodations in the shopping, sightseeing, restaurants district of Hong Kong, Hotel Icon is a popular pick for both business and leisure travelers. Only 25.2 km away, this 5-star hotel can be easily accessed from the airport. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

The facilities and services provided by Hotel Icon ensure a pleasant stay for guests. 24-hour room service, free Wi-Fi in all rooms, car power charging station, convenience store, daily housekeeping are just a few of the facilities that set Hotel Icon apart from other hotels in the city.

Hotel accommodations have been carefully appointed to the highest degree of comfort and convenience. In some of the rooms, guests can find television LCD/plasma screen, slippers, smoke detector, internet access – wireless, internet access – wireless (complimentary). The hotel offers wonderful recreational facilities such as hot tub, fitness center, sauna, outdoor pool, spa to make your stay truly unforgettable. With an ideal location and facilities to match, Hotel Icon hits the spot in many ways.

Guest policies
