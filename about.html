About Primas Travel

Primas Travel started up in 2004, and we’ve been on an epic ride ever since. From our beginnings as a local travel agency company to launching our online brand globally in 2007, we’ve tried to stay true to our core beliefs to deliver an exceptional experience for our community and users. We owe a huge thanks to our community for joining us on this awesome journey, and we hope that you’ll continue to be a part of our story.
