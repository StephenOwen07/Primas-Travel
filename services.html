How it works

Step 1: Choose how many users

Choose your specific plan based on how many users you want. Starter plan: up to 2 users, Family plan: up to 4 users and Premium plan: unlimited users.

Step 2: Choose your destinations

Choose which counries and cities you desire to visit based on which price plan you choose.

Any destination you can think of is possible. Our team will help you to find the right destination for your trip

Step 3: Choose your accommodation and activites

Choose which acommadation type you wish to stay in based upon your budget. From 5 star hotels, air bnb or even hostels, we will find the right accomadation for your needs.

Our team will help you choose and customise what activites you wish to do. From ziplining across Lake Tahoe to. dining at the Marina Bay Sands hotel, we will find the activities which suite your trip the most.

Step 4: Digital documentation

Once you are happy with your trips customization, we will organize all of your documentation for you, taking the hassle away from you.

Your documentation will be digitally available so you can view it on whichever device suits you. No more thrifting through tedius documentation.

Frequentley Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard and American Express. We also accept online methods such as Paypal and Stripe. For enterprise customers we also offer an option to pay by invoice.

What if i have a problem on our trip?

We offer 24/7 support everyday. If you encounter any problems you can contact us on our customer support number +65 7461 6556 and we will ensure that the situation is fully resolved.

Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes you can upgrade your plan at anytime, even during your trip. You cannot downgrade your plan during your trip due booking costs.

Can i pay in installations?

At this time we do not currently offer monthly or finance payment options.

Can i get a refund?

You can get a refund before you have left for your trip. If for any reason you are not happy with accomadation we will find similar alternative accomadation. If you are not happy with your activities then refunds are offered on a case by case basis. Contact our customer support for more information +65 7461 6556.

What if a member is injured on our trip?

Our travel insurance fully covers all members, so you can rest assured that you will recieve the best treatment possible
