Marina Bay Sands overview

Whether you're a tourist or traveling on business, Ximen Taipei DreamHouse is a great choice for accommodation when visiting Taipei. Only 7.6 km away, this 2-star hotel can be easily accessed from the airport. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

The facilities and services provided by Ximen Taipei DreamHouse ensure a pleasant stay for guests. The hotel provides free Wi-Fi in all rooms, convenience store, daily housekeeping, taxi service, luggage storage to ensure our guests the greatest comfort.

Experience high quality room facilities during your stay here. Some rooms include television LCD/plasma screen, towels, internet access – wireless, internet access – wireless (complimentary), non smoking rooms, provided to help guests recharge after a long day. Besides, the hotel's host of recreational offerings ensures you have plenty to do during your stay. Ximen Taipei DreamHouse is an ideal place of stay for travelers seeking charm, comfort and convenience in Taipei.
