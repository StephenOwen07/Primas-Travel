More about Fairytale YiSu Hot Spring Hotel

Fairytale YiSu Hot Spring Hotel overview

Located in the lovely area of Jiaoxi Township, Fairytale YiSu Hot Spring Hotel enjoys a commanding position in the sightseeing, spas, family fun hub of Yilan. Set 8KM from the excitement of the city, this 3-star hotel commands an excellent location and provides access to the city's biggest attractions. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

At Fairytale YiSu Hot Spring Hotel, the excellent service and superior facilities make for an unforgettable stay. Free Wi-Fi in all rooms, daily housekeeping, grocery deliveries, taxi service, 24-hour front desk are just a few of the facilities that set Fairytale YiSu Hot Spring Hotel apart from other hotels in the city.

50 rooms spread over 12 floors provide a warm and pleasant home away from home. Modern comforts such as television LCD/plasma screen, carpeting, cleaning products, clothes rack, complimentary instant coffee can be found in selected rooms. The hotel's hot spring bath, children's playground are ideal places to relax and unwind after a busy day. Enjoy unparalleled services and a truly prestigious address at the Fairytale YiSu Hot Spring Hotel.
